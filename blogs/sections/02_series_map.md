## The Series: Building Trusted Enterprise Context in Databricks

The arc is:

1. **Discover and Domains**: organize trusted assets by business context.
2. **Metric Views**: define certified business KPIs.
3. **Advanced Metric Semantics**: model level of detail, windows, and AI-friendly metadata.
4. **Metric Views in Production**: use materialization to keep trusted semantic definitions fast.
5. **Glossary and Operating Model**: govern business terms, ownership, certification, review, and lifecycle.

This post focuses on the first layer: **Discover and Domains**.

The materialization topic will appear later as the production-readiness chapter. Performance matters, but only after the organization has agreed which context and definitions should be trusted.
