# Building Trusted Context in Databricks Part 1: Discover and Domains

Subtitle:

How Discover and Domains create the first business-facing layer of trusted context for AI/BI, Genie, dashboards, and enterprise analytics.

Audience:

Data leaders, platform teams, governance teams, analytics engineers, and AI/BI practitioners building trusted semantic metadata on Databricks.

Core takeaway:

Trusted AI/BI starts before a query is written. Discover and Domains help people, BI tools, and agents find the right business context, understand which assets are certified, and avoid stale or deprecated sources.
